## Executive summary

Aziron proposes a phased cloud-native modernization aligned to Acme’s requirements for security, scalability, and time-to-value. Our approach combines fixed-price milestones with transparent change control.

## Understanding of requirements

Acme seeks:

1. Replacement of monolithic services with modular APIs  
2. Enterprise SSO and role-based access  
3. Observable, auditable operations from day one  
4. Predictable commercial model with milestone-based billing  

## Proposed solution

| Capability | Approach |
|------------|----------|
| Identity | SAML/OIDC SSO, RBAC, audit logs |
| Integration | REST + event-driven adapters |
| Operations | Metrics, tracing, runbooks |
| Delivery | 4-week discovery + 12-week build |

## Commercial summary

- **Phase 1 (Discovery):** $48,000 — 4 weeks  
- **Phase 2 (Build):** $112,000 — 12 weeks
