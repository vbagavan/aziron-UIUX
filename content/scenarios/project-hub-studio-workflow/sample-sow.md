# Statement of Work — Acme Platform Modernization

**SOW ID:** SOW-ACME-2026-014  
**Version:** 2.1  
**Effective date:** 1 July 2026  
**Client:** Acme Corporation  
**Vendor:** Aziron Professional Services  

---

## 1. Background

Acme Corporation is replacing legacy on-premise systems with a cloud-native platform. This SOW defines scope, deliverables, milestones, and commercial terms for Phase 1 (Discovery) and Phase 2 (Core Build).

## 2. Scope of work

### 2.1 Phase 1 — Discovery & architecture (4 weeks)

- Current-state assessment and integration inventory  
- Target architecture and security model  
- Milestone plan and RAID log  

**Deliverables:** Architecture decision record (ADR), refined backlog, signed milestone plan  

**Fee:** $48,000 (fixed)  

### 2.2 Phase 2 — Core platform build (12 weeks)

- Identity and access (SSO, RBAC)  
- API layer and event bus  
- Admin console and observability baseline  

**Deliverables:** Production-ready MVP per acceptance criteria in Appendix A  

**Fee:** $112,000 (fixed)  

### 2.3 Out of scope

- Data migration from legacy ERP (separate CR)  
- 24/7 managed operations (optional SLA add-on)  

## 3. Milestone schedule & payment

| Milestone | Acceptance | Amount | Terms |
|-----------|------------|--------|-------|
| M1 — Discovery complete | Signed ADR + plan | $48,000 | Net 30 |
| M2 — MVP go-live | Appendix A checklist | $112,000 | Net 30 |

## 4. Change control

Scope changes require a written Change Request (CR) signed by both parties per the Master Service Agreement.

## 5. Assumptions

- Acme provides sandbox access within 5 business days of kickoff  
- Single production region (US-West) unless CR approved
